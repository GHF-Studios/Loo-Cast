Product Constellation is the system-level product relationship model that treats game, engine, and creator ecosystem
as one coherent compositional product space rather than isolated products.
At this layer, [[Loo Cast]] is the playable experience, [[Spacetime Engine]] is the host/runtime core, and
[[Modding Ecosystem]] is the creator expansion surface.
The strategic product direction is that these remain mutually reinforcing along the [[Player-to-Creator Path]] under
the commitments of [[Project Ethos]].
Role-level dependency shape across these pillars is tracked in [[Pillar Dependency Topology]].
