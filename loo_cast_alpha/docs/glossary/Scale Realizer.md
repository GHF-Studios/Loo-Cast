A Scale Realizer is the semantic realization concept bound to one [[Scale Slice]].
It is the concept formerly referred to as a "phenomena realizer", renamed to keep scale-centric terminology coherent.
Each active scale slice must resolve to exactly one effective scale realizer as constrained by
[[Scale Realizer Cardinality]].
