Scale Support is the declared support state of one capability at one [[Scale]] coordinate.
Each capability-scale pair has exactly one support state: `supported` or `unsupported`.
Scale-support declaration and validation rules are governed by the [[Scale Contract]] and consumed by the
[[Capability Runtime]].
