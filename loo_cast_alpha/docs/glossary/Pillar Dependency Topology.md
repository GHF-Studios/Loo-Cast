The Pillar Dependency Topology maps role-level dependencies across the [[Loo Cast]], the [[Spacetime Engine]], and
the [[Modding Ecosystem]].
The [[Loo Cast]] depends on both the [[Spacetime Engine]] and the [[Modding Ecosystem]].
The [[Spacetime Engine]] depends on the [[Modding Ecosystem]] through implemented modding contracts.
Pillars remain replaceable when replacements satisfy the relevant contracts within the [[Product Constellation]].
